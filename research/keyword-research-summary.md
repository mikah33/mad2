This comprehensive keyword research reveals significant opportunities in the Columbia, SC car detailing market with over 325,000+ monthly searches for location-based detailing keywords alone. ## Competitor Analysis & Insights

### What Top-Ranking Detailing Sites Are Doing:

#### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization (CRITICAL)
- **Complete every field** with accurate NAP data
- **3-5 new photos weekly** (before/after shots prioritized)
- **Weekly posts** about services, specials, tips
- **Active review generation** and response (within 24 hours)
- **Service categories**: Select all relevant categories
- **Q&A section**: Proactively answer common questions

**Impact:** Reviews account for 16% of local pack ranking factors

#### 2. Local SEO Domination
**Strategy:**
- Target city/neighborhood-specific keywords
- Create individual location pages for each service area
- Use local schema markup (LocalBusiness, Service)
- Build citations in local directories
- Get listed in car forums and local service apps

**Top Local Keywords:**
- "car detailing [neighborhood]"
- "ceramic coating [city]"
- "[service] near [landmark]"

#### 3. High-Quality Visual Content
**Requirements:**
- Before/after photo galleries (minimum 50+ photos)
- Video demonstrations (YouTube for SEO)
- Time-lapse transformation videos
- Service process documentation
- Customer testimonial videos

**SEO Benefit:** Images with proper alt text, video content ranks in search

#### 4. Educational Content Strategy
**Format:**
- 2-4 blog posts monthly (800-1200 words)
- Service comparison articles
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Industry news and trends
- Seasonal car care tips

**Top Performing Content Types:**
- "How Long Does [Service] Last?"
- "[Service 1] vs [Service 2]: Which is Right for You?"
- "5 Signs Your Car Needs [Service]"
- "Complete Guide to [Service]"

**Timeline:** SEO takes 3-4 months to see results, 6-12 months for significant growth

#### 5. Mobile-First Technical Optimization
**Requirements:**
- **Core Web Vitals**: LCP <2.5s, FID <100ms, CLS <0.1
- **Mobile responsive design**: 80%+ searches are mobile
- **Click-to-call buttons** prominently displayed
- **Easy online booking** system
- **Fast image loading**: WebP format, lazy loading
