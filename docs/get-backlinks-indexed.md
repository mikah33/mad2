# How to Get Your Backlinks Indexed by Google

## The Problem
You can't submit external URLs (Perplexity, SlideShare, Dev.to, etc.) to Google Search Console.

## The Solution: 4 Proven Methods

---

## Method 1: Social Media Sharing (Easiest) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**How It Works:**
Google's crawlers monitor social media 24/7. When you share a link on social platforms, Google discovers and indexes it within 1-7 days.

**What To Do:**
1. Share your backlink URLs on:
   - Facebook (business page + personal)
   - Twitter/X
   - LinkedIn
   - Pinterest
   - Reddit (relevant subreddits)

2. Example Post:
```
📝 New Guide: Mobile Auto Detailing in Columbia SC

Everything you need to know about professional mobile detailing,
pricing, and what to expect. Check it out!

[Link to your Perplexity/Issuu/SlideShare page]

#ColumbiaS #AutoDetailing #MobileDetailing
```

**Pro Tip:** Share each backlink 2-3 times across different platforms

**Timeline:** Indexed in 1-7 days

---

## Method 2: Create a "Resources" Page on Your Site ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**How It Works:**
When you link to external pages from YOUR site, Google's crawler follows those links during regular site crawls.

**What To Do:**

Create a new page: `/resources` or `/media-mentions`

**Content Example:**
```markdown
# Auto Detailing Resources & Guides

## Our Educational Content

We've published comprehensive guides on auto detailing across the web:

- [Mobile Auto Detailing Columbia SC - Complete Guide](perplexity-url)
- [Professional Car Detailing Tips](dev-to-url)
- [Ceramic Coating vs Traditional Wax](slideshare-url)
- [Interior Car Detailing Best Practices](issuu-url)

## Industry Resources

- Auto Detailing Industry Association
- Professional Detailers Alliance
- etc.
```

**Why This Works:**
- Your site gets crawled regularly by Google
- Google follows all outbound links
- Your backlink pages get discovered automatically

**Timeline:** Indexed in 3-14 days

---

## Method 3: Use IndexNow API (Advanced) ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**How It Works:**
IndexNow is a protocol that notifies search engines (Bing, Yandex) instantly about new URLs. Bing shares data with Google.

**What To Do:**

1. Go to: https://www.indexnow.org
2. Submit your backlink URLs one by one
3. Bing indexes them immediately
4. Google discovers via Bing's crawl data

**Free Tools:**
- https://www.seoreviewtools.com/indexnow-ping/
- https://www.rankwatch.com/indexnow-url-submission/

**Timeline:** Indexed in 1-3 days

---

## Method 4: Link Building from Backlinks ⭐⭐⭐

**How It Works:**
Link your backlink pages to EACH OTHER, creating a web of connections.

**What To Do:**

On your Perplexity page, add links to:
- Your Dev.to articles
- Your SlideShare presentations
- Your Issuu documents

On your Dev.to articles, link to:
- Your Perplexity pages
- Your SlideShare docs

**Why This Works:**
Google's crawler follows link chains. If it discovers ONE page, it follows all links on that page.

**Timeline:** Indexed in 7-21 days

---

## Method 5: Ping Services (Quick & Easy) ⭐⭐⭐

**How It Works:**
Ping services notify search engines about new content.

**Free Ping Services:**
- http://pingomatic.com
- https://pingler.com
- https://www.pingmyurl.com

**What To Do:**
1. Go to ping service
2. Paste your backlink URL
3. Submit
4. Service notifies Google/Bing

**Timeline:** Indexed in 3-10 days

---

## Method 6: RSS Feed Submission ⭐⭐

**For Platforms with RSS:**
- Dev.to articles have RSS feeds
- Medium posts have RSS feeds

**What To Do:**
1. Find the RSS feed URL (usually /feed or /rss)
2. Submit to: https://feedburner.google.com
3. Submit to: https://www.feedage.com

**Timeline:** Indexed in 5-14 days

## How to Check if Backlinks Are Indexed

### Method 1: Site Search
```
site:perplexity.ai mobile auto detailing columbia
site:dev.to mikah auto detailing
site:slideshare.net mikah's auto detailing
```

If your page appears, it's indexed!

### Method 2: Direct URL Search
Paste the full URL in Google search. If it shows up, it's indexed.

### Method 3: Google Search Console (for YOUR site)
Go to: Coverage → Valid
Shows all indexed pages from YOUR domain

## Pro Tips

1. **Prioritize High-Authority Backlinks**
   Index these first:
   - Perplexity (DA 85)
   - Dev.to (DA 88)
   - SlideShare (DA 95)
   - Reddit (DA 91)

2. **Share Multiple Times**
   Don't just share once. Reshare after 1 week if not indexed.

3. **Use Different Anchor Text**
   When linking from your Resources page, vary the text:
   - "Comprehensive mobile detailing guide"
   - "Learn about ceramic coating"
   - "Professional detailing tips"

4. **Monitor Progress**
   Check weekly: Which pages are indexed? Which aren't?
   Focus efforts on non-indexed pages.

5. **Quality Over Speed**
   Better to have 20 indexed backlinks than 100 un-indexed ones.
