## Page Template Structure

Each neighborhood page should follow this structure:

### Meta Information
```
Title: Mobile Auto Detailing in [Neighborhood], [City] SC | Mikah's Mobile Detailing
Description: Professional mobile auto detailing serving [Neighborhood] in [City], SC. Ceramic coating, interior detailing, paint correction. We come to your [Neighborhood] home. Call (803) 667-8731.
```

### H1 Heading
```
Mobile Auto Detailing in [Neighborhood], [City] SC
```

### Page Sections

1. **Hero Section**
   - Neighborhood-specific headline
   - "Serving [Neighborhood] Residents" badge
   - CTA: "Book Your [Neighborhood] Detail"

2. **Introduction (150+ words, unique)**
   - Mention neighborhood by name 3-4 times
   - Reference local landmarks/features
   - Highlight why residents choose mobile detailing

3. **Services Section**
   - All services with links
   - Neighborhood-specific benefits per service
   - Pricing tiers

4. **Why [Neighborhood] Residents Choose Us**
   - Convenience for their lifestyle
   - Local testimonials (if available)
   - Specific neighborhood considerations

5. **Service Area Details**
   - Streets/sections covered
   - Adjacent neighborhoods served
   - Map embed centered on neighborhood

6. **Neighborhood-Specific FAQ (5 questions)**
   - "Do you service [Neighborhood]?"
   - "Where do you detail cars in [Neighborhood]?"
   - "Is there a travel fee for [Neighborhood]?"
   - Neighborhood-specific questions

7. **CTA Section**
   - Phone number
   - Book online button
   - Contact form

8. **Internal Links**
   - Link to parent city page
   - Link to 2-3 adjacent neighborhoods
   - Link to all services

## Content Differentiation by Neighborhood Type

### Gated Communities (Reserve at Midway, Murray Forest, Spring Valley)
- Emphasize trusted service provider status
- Mention working with HOA requirements
- Premium/concierge service language
- Flexible scheduling for gate access

### Lakefront/Boat Communities (Timberlake, Night Harbor, Palmetto Shores, Secret Cove)
- Boat + vehicle combo packages
- Lake pollen/algae removal expertise
- Trailer/watercraft detailing services
- Seasonal lake lifestyle content

### Historic Affluent (Heathwood, Shandon, Arcadia Lakes)
- Classic car expertise
- Luxury vehicle specialization
- Estate/driveway convenience
- Old-money quality service language

### Family Suburbs (Chestnut Hill, Chelsea Park, Eagles Rest, Cobblestone Farms)
- Family fleet packages
- Child seat sanitization
- Pet hair removal expertise
- Multi-vehicle discounts

### New Construction (River Shoals, Palmetto Shores, Boykin Hills)
- New vehicle protection packages
- Ceramic coating for new cars
- Construction dust removal
- First-time homeowner specials
