# Auto Detailing SEO Checklist 2026

*(Customized for mobile auto detailing services)*

## #1. Title Tag Optimization for Auto Detailing

**Formula:** `[Primary Service] + [Location] + [Unique Value] + [Year/Numbers]`

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Mobile Auto Detailing Lexington SC 2026 | Same-Day Service"
✅ "Car Detailing Near Me Columbia SC | 5-Star Rated Since 2020"
✅ "Ceramic Coating Lexington SC | 7-Year Protection Guarantee"
```

**Rules:**
- Keep under 60 characters
- Lead with primary keyword
- Include specific location (neighborhood/city)
- Add unique value prop (same-day, 5-star, guarantee)
- Consider seasonal keywords (summer car care, winter prep)

## #2. Meta Descriptions for Local Auto Services

**Formula:** `[Service] in [Location]. [Specific Benefits]. [Social Proof]. [Clear CTA].`

**Examples:**
```
✅ "Mobile auto detailing in Lexington SC. Paint correction, ceramic coating & interior deep cleaning at your location. 200+ 5-star reviews. Book your free quote today!"

✅ "Professional car detailing near Columbia SC. We come to you with all equipment & water. Same-day service available. Get spotless results - call now for instant quote!"
```

**Rules:**
- 155-160 characters exactly
- Include primary keyword in first sentence
- Mention specific services
- Add location + "near me" variations
- Include social proof (reviews/years)
- End with strong CTA

## #3. Content Structure for Auto Detailing

**H1 Formula:** `[Service] in [Location] | [Unique Benefit]`
```
✅ "Mobile Auto Detailing in Lexington SC | We Come to You"
✅ "Ceramic Coating Columbia SC | 7-Year Paint Protection"
```

**H2 Structure:**
```
- Why Choose [Business Name] for [Service]?
- [Service] Process & What's Included
- Pricing & Packages for [Location] Residents
- Before & After Gallery
- Customer Reviews & Testimonials
- Service Areas & Scheduling
- Frequently Asked Questions
```

## #4. URL Structure for Auto Services

**Best Practices:**
```
✅ /mobile-auto-detailing-lexington-sc/
✅ /ceramic-coating-columbia/
✅ /car-wash-near-me/
✅ /paint-correction-services/

❌ /services/automotive/mobile-detailing-services-2026/
❌ /car-detailing-page/
```

**Rules:**
- Include primary keyword + location
- Use hyphens, not underscores
- Keep under 5 words
- Avoid stop words (and, the, of)
- Match title tag keywords

## #5. Auto Detailing Image Optimization

**File Naming:**
```
✅ mobile-car-detailing-lexington-sc-before-after.jpg
✅ ceramic-coating-application-columbia.jpg
✅ interior-detailing-lexington-results.jpg

❌ IMG_2847.jpg
❌ photo1.png
```

**Alt Text Examples:**
```
✅ "Mobile auto detailing service in Lexington SC showing before and after paint correction results"
✅ "Professional ceramic coating application on luxury car in Columbia SC"
✅ "Interior car detailing results showing deep cleaned leather seats in Lexington"
```

**Rules:**
- Include service + location in filename
- Describe actual service being performed
- Mention location naturally in alt text
- Use descriptive, keyword-rich captions

## #6. Internal Linking Strategy for Auto Services

**Service Page Links:**
- Link each service to related services (ceramic coating → paint correction)
- Link to location pages (Lexington, Columbia, Irmo)
- Link to relevant FAQ sections
- Link to pricing pages

**Anchor Text Examples:**
```
✅ "professional paint correction services"
✅ "ceramic coating in Columbia SC"
✅ "mobile detailing near Lexington"

❌ "click here"
❌ "read more"
❌ "services"
```

**Link Strategy:**
- 3-5 internal links per page
- Link high-converting pages to each other
- Use location + service keywords as anchors
- Link to customer testimonials

## #7. Content Optimization for Auto Detailing

**First 100 Words Must Include:**
- Primary keyword (mobile auto detailing)
- Location (Lexington SC)
- Unique value proposition

**Content Length Targets:**
