Comprehensive Mobile Detailing Services

Mikah's Auto Detailing offers complete mobile detailing packages designed for South Carolina's demanding climate. Our Basic Detail Package ($150-200) includes professional hand washing using pH-balanced solutions and proper two-bucket method, premium carnauba wax or synthetic sealant protection, complete interior vacuuming reaching all surfaces and crevices, dashboard and console cleaning with UV protectant, window cleaning inside and out, and tire dressing for showroom appearance. This service takes 2-3 hours and suits well-maintained vehicles needing regular maintenance between comprehensive details.

Our Complete Detail Package ($250-350) represents our most popular service level, providing comprehensive care that most Columbia vehicles need quarterly. This package includes everything in Basic Detail plus clay bar decontamination removing bonded contaminants that washing alone cannot eliminate, single-stage paint polishing removing light swirl marks and surface imperfections, premium sealant providing six months of UV and environmental protection, carpet and upholstery shampooing using commercial hot water extraction, leather cleaning and conditioning preventing cracking in South Carolina's heat, and complete interior restoration addressing normal wear and accumulation. This service requires 4-5 hours and delivers results maintaining vehicle condition year-round despite Columbia's challenging environment.

For customers seeking maximum protection and restoration, our Premium Detail Package ($400-600) provides comprehensive paint correction, ceramic spray coating, and intensive interior restoration. Services include multi-stage paint correction using progressive compounds and pads to remove heavy swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation accumulated over years, ceramic spray coating providing 6-12 months of hydrophobic protection against UV exposure, water spotting, and environmental contaminants, complete interior deep cleaning with steam extraction and antimicrobial treatment, odor elimination for smoke, pets, or mildew using enzymatic cleaners and ozone treatment when necessary, headlight restoration improving visibility and appearance, and engine bay detailing when requested. This package takes 6-8+ hours and addresses significant restoration needs or provides maximum protection for vehicle investments.

Specialized Services and Add-Ons

Beyond standard packages, Mikah's Auto Detailing offers specialized services addressing specific concerns common throughout Columbia and Lexington County vehicles. Our pet hair removal service ($50-100) uses commercial-grade equipment including rubber brushes, pumice stones, and powerful vacuums extracting embedded hair from carpets, upholstery, and crevices throughout the vehicle. Smoke odor elimination ($100-200) combines enzymatic cleaning of all surfaces, hot water extraction removing smoke particles from upholstery, ozone treatment when necessary, and complete air vent sanitization—eliminating odors at the molecular level rather than masking them with fragrances.

Heavy stain removal ($75-150) addresses stubborn stains that basic cleaning cannot eliminate, with our technicians identifying stain types and applying appropriate removal techniques for coffee and soda spills, mud and ground-in dirt, grease and oil, ink and dye transfer, and food stains. We also accommodate oversized vehicles including large SUVs, trucks, and commercial vans with appropriate surcharges ($50-100) reflecting additional time and materials required for larger surface areas.
