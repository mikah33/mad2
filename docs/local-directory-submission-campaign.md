## Submission Best Practices

### Profile Optimization Standards
1. **Consistent NAP**: Exact same business name, address format, phone
2. **Professional Description**: Use the same optimized description across all platforms
3. **Category Selection**: Choose most relevant primary and secondary categories
4. **Photo Strategy**: Upload 3-5 optimized photos per platform
5. **Service Areas**: Clearly specify all covered locations
6. **Hours**: Indicate flexible/mobile service availability

### Standard Business Description Template
```
Mikah's Auto Detailing provides professional mobile car detailing services throughout Columbia SC, Lexington SC, and surrounding areas. We offer same-day service with expert interior and exterior detailing, ceramic coating, paint correction, and more. IDA certified, fully insured, and with 31+ five-star reviews. Call (803) 667-8731 for your free quote!
```

### Photo Upload Strategy
- **Logo**: Use optimized logo.jpg (consistent branding)
- **Before/After**: 2-3 transformation photos
- **Service Photos**: Detailing process shots
- **Vehicle Types**: Show variety of cars, SUVs, trucks served
- **Mobile Setup**: Show professional mobile equipment
