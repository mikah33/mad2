## Frequently Asked Questions

### What areas do you serve?
We provide mobile auto detailing services throughout the Phoenix metropolitan area, including Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, and surrounding cities within a 25-mile radius.

### How long does a typical detail take?
Most detailing services take between 2-4 hours depending on the package and vehicle condition. More intensive services like ceramic coating or paint correction may take 4-8 hours or require multiple days.

### Do I need to provide water or electricity?
No! Our mobile units are completely self-contained with their own water supply and power generators. We just need access to your vehicle.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and PayPal. Payment is due upon completion of service.

### How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, though we can often accommodate same-day or next-day appointments based on availability.

### Do you offer any warranties or guarantees?
Yes! We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Ceramic coatings come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 2-7 years depending on the product.
