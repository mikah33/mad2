### Ceramic Coating
**Pricing: $599-$1,299 | Duration: 1-2 days**

Ceramic coating represents the pinnacle of paint protection technology. Unlike traditional waxes that last weeks or sealants that last months, ceramic coatings provide years of protection with superior durability and self-cleaning properties.

We are authorized installers of Ceramic Pro, one of the industry's leading ceramic coating brands, offering warranties from 2 to 7 years depending on the package selected.

What is ceramic coating?
Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent or semi-permanent layer of protection. The coating creates a hydrophobic (water-repelling) surface that makes your vehicle easier to clean and more resistant to environmental damage.

Benefits:
- Multi-year protection (2-7 year warranties available)
- UV protection prevents paint fading and oxidation
- Chemical resistance protects against bird droppings, bug splatter, acid rain
- Hydrophobic properties cause water to bead and sheet off
- Self-cleaning effect reduces dirt accumulation
- Enhanced gloss and depth far superior to wax
- Scratch resistance (reduces minor swirls and scratches)
- Reduced washing frequency and easier cleaning
- Maintains resale value by preserving paint condition
- No need for waxing ever again

Our ceramic coating process:
1. Thorough wash and decontamination (iron remover, clay bar)
2. Paint correction to remove all imperfections (if needed, included in premium packages)
3. Paint surface preparation with isopropyl alcohol wipe-down
4. Multiple layers of ceramic coating applied by hand (2-5 layers depending on package)
5. Proper curing time between layers (can't be rushed)
6. Wheels and calipers can be coated for easier cleaning
7. Windows can be treated with hydrophobic coating
8. Final curing period (24-48 hours before water exposure)
9. Maintenance kit and care instructions provided

Ceramic coating packages:
- Bronze Package: 2-year warranty, single layer ($599)
- Silver Package: 3-year warranty, two layers ($799)
- Gold Package: 5-year warranty, three layers + wheels ($999)
- Platinum Package: 7-year warranty, five layers + wheels + windows + paint correction ($1,299)

Maintenance: While ceramic coating reduces maintenance significantly, we offer annual maintenance packages to ensure your coating stays in optimal condition and maintains warranty requirements.

Perfect for: New vehicles, vehicles with excellent paint, long-term protection, reducing maintenance time, maximizing resale value

### Paint Correction
**Pricing: $399-$899 | Duration: 4-8 hours**

Paint correction is a process that removes imperfections in your vehicle's paint through a multi-stage polishing process. Over time, improper washing techniques, automated car washes, and environmental factors create swirl marks, scratches, water spots, and oxidation that dull your paint's appearance.

Paint correction issues we address:
- Swirl marks and spider webbing (most common)
- Light to moderate scratches
- Oxidation and paint fading
- Water spots and etching
- Holograms from improper polishing
- Orange peel texture (to some degree)
- Bird dropping and bug etching
- Clear coat defects

Our paint correction process:
1. Complete wash and decontamination (iron remover, clay bar)
2. Paint depth measurement using a paint thickness gauge (ensures safety)
3. Test spot to determine correct polishing compounds and pads
4. Paint inspection under high-intensity LED lighting
5. Tape and mask trim to prevent polishing compound buildup
6. Multi-stage polishing:
   - Stage 1: Heavy cutting compound to remove deeper imperfections (if needed)
   - Stage 2: Medium polish to refine surface
   - Stage 3: Fine polish for final clarity and gloss
7. Each panel is inspected with LED lights between stages
8. All polishing residue removed
9. Paint protection applied (wax, sealant, or ceramic coating recommended)
10. Final inspection in sunlight and LED lighting

Paint correction levels:
- Single Stage ($399-$499): Removes 50-60% of defects, enhances gloss
- Two Stage ($599-$699): Removes 70-85% of defects, significant improvement
- Multi Stage ($799-$899): Removes 90-95%+ of defects, show car finish

Equipment we use:
- Dual-action and rotary polishers (Rupes, Flex)
- Professional-grade compounds and polishes (Meguiar's, 3D, Sonax)
- Various foam and microfiber pads for different paint hardness
- Paint thickness gauge for safety
- High-intensity LED inspection lights

Important note: Paint correction removes a thin layer of clear coat. We carefully measure paint thickness to ensure we never compromise the integrity of your paint. Deep scratches that go through clear coat to primer or base coat cannot be fully removed without repainting.

Paint correction is often combined with ceramic coating for maximum results - the correction provides a perfect surface, and the coating locks in that perfection for years.

Perfect for: Restoring used vehicles, preparing for ceramic coating, removing years of wash damage, pre-sale preparation, show car finish

### Headlight Restoration
**Pricing: $79-$129 | Duration: 1 hour**

Over time, UV exposure causes plastic headlight lenses to become foggy, yellowed, and hazy, significantly reducing visibility and making your vehicle look older. Our headlight restoration process removes oxidation and re-protects the lens.

Process:

- Machine polishing for optical clarity

- Final buffing

Results: Like-new clarity, improved nighttime visibility, better appearance. Results typically last 1-2 years before UV protection requires reapplication.

### Results and Maintenance

**Q: How long will the detailing last?**
A: This depends on many factors: how you maintain it, where the vehicle is parked, weather conditions, and driving frequency. With proper maintenance (regular washes, parking in shade/garage when possible), detailing results can last 2-4 months. Ceramic coating results last years with proper care.

**Q: How should I maintain my vehicle after detailing?**
A:
- Wait 48 hours before washing (allows products to fully cure)
- Use pH-neutral car soap (never dish soap)
- Wash using the two-bucket method or touchless wash
- Avoid automated brush car washes (they cause swirls)
- Dry with microfiber towels, never air dry
- Park in shade or garage when possible
- Address bird droppings and bug splatter promptly
- We provide care instructions and product recommendations

**Q: Can I take my car through an automatic car wash after detailing?**
A: We strongly discourage automated brush car washes as they cause swirl marks that undo our paint correction work. Touchless automatic washes are acceptable but hand washing or professional detailing is always best. If you have ceramic coating, touchless washes work fine for maintenance between details.

**Q: Do you offer maintenance services?**
A: Yes! After your initial detail or ceramic coating, we offer maintenance wash services to keep your vehicle looking great between full details. Maintenance washes are quicker and less expensive than full detailing.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the results?**

### Seasonal Questions

**Q: Is there a best time of year to detail my car?**
A: Spring and fall are popular times, but there's never a bad time to detail your vehicle. Each season has benefits:
- Spring: Remove winter salt, pollen, and prepare for warm weather
- Summer: Protect against UV damage and heat
- Fall: Prepare for winter and protect against leaves/sap
- Winter: Remove road salt and grime regularly

**Q: Should I get ceramic coating before winter or summer?**
A: Either time works, but many customers prefer fall application to protect against winter salt, ice, and harsh conditions. Summer application protects against UV rays, heat, and summer storms. Really, any time is fine!

**Q: How do you deal with pollen season in Columbia?**
A: Pollen season in the Carolinas is intense! We recommend more frequent maintenance washes during pollen season (March-May). Ceramic coating helps significantly as pollen doesn't stick as readily to the hydrophobic surface and rinses off more easily.

**Q: Can you detail in winter?**
A: Yes! While we prefer temperatures above 50°F for exterior work (especially ceramic coating), we can detail in winter. Interior services can be performed year-round. We may reschedule exterior work if it's below freezing or during ice storms.

Protect against intense UV rays, heat, tree sap, and bug splatter. Interiors need attention from heat and sun exposure.

Recommended services:
- Regular maintenance washes (every 2 weeks)
- Interior cleaning and UV protection
- Convertible top cleaning and protection
- Bug and tar removal
- Headlight restoration (prevents UV yellowing)

**Fall (September-November):**
Remove summer buildup, prepare for winter, apply protective coatings before harsh weather.

Recommended services:
- Full exterior detail
- Paint protection (wax or ceramic coating)
- Interior detail before winter tracking begins
- Wheel protection (easier winter cleaning)
- Ideal time for ceramic coating application

**Winter (December-February):**
Combat road salt, ice melt, mud, and shorter days. More frequent maintenance prevents salt damage.

Recommended services:
- Regular maintenance washes including undercarriage
- Interior cleaning (salt, mud, moisture)
- Ceramic coating helps repel salt and ice melt
- Quick detailing between snow events

## Product Recommendations for DIY Maintenance

Between professional detailing, maintain your vehicle with quality products:

**For Washing:**
- Meguiar's Gold Class or Chemical Guys Mr. Pink soap
- Two buckets (one wash, one rinse)
- Microfiber wash mitts (not sponges)
- Grit guards for buckets

**For Drying:**
- Large microfiber drying towels
- Leaf blower for hard-to-reach areas (crevices, mirrors)

**For Interior:**
- Vacuum with crevice tools
- Interior quick detailer spray
- Microfiber towels for dusting
- Leather conditioner (for leather only)

**For Wheels:**
- Wheel-specific cleaner (acid-free)
- Wheel brushes
- Separate wash mitt for wheels

**What NOT to Use:**
- Dish soap (strips wax/protection)
- Harsh cleaners (can damage surfaces)
- Household glass cleaner on tinted windows
- Armor All (can cause dashboards to crack)
- Automated brush car washes

We're happy to recommend specific products based on your vehicle and needs!
